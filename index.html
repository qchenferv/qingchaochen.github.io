Biography

My research focus on the intersaction of electronic engineering, computer science and computational clinical research, with special interests in transfer learning, deep learning, human sensing using multi-modal sensors and machine learning framework. My research goal is to understand how to integrate multi-modal sensor design and machine learning framework to assist, understand and intervene clinical practice. I am especially interested in understanding the cognitive process of clinical diagnosis based on observational data

I am leading the MSPI Group.

Research Interests

  • Radar Sensor Design and Processing: passive wifi radar, active MIMO radar, cognitive radar with vision,human sensing and chronical disease sensing
  • Computer Vision and Machine Learning: transfer learning, domain adaptation, video-text retrieval
  • Multi-sensor learning for clinical applications: multi-modal machine learning, eye-tracking enabled machine learning, ultrasound video analysis, histological image analysis
